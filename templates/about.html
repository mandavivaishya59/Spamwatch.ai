About SpamWatch AI

Our Objective

At SpamWatch AI, our mission is to create a safer digital space by empowering everyone to detect and block fake and spam emails, deepfakes, and fraudulent messages in real time. In an age of misinformation and cyber threats, our goal is simple — make trust easy and fraud detection smarter using advanced AI.

What is SpamWatch AI?

SpamWatch AI is an all-in-one intelligent tool that analyzes your emails, messages, videos, and images to verify their authenticity. Whether you’re a student, professional, journalist, or everyday internet user — SpamWatch AI helps you separate truth from deception in seconds.

Our Tools

1️⃣ Spam Email Detector

Upload or paste any suspicious email. Our AI scans headers, language, links, and attachments to detect phishing or scam emails with a clear authenticity score.

2️⃣ Fake WhatsApp Message Detector

Paste the message. SpamWatch AI analyzes text patterns, sources, and context to spot fake or manipulated messages instantly.

3️⃣ Deepfake Image & Video Detector

Upload an image or video to check for AI-generated deepfakes. Our system uses facial recognition and deep learning to flag synthetic or manipulated visuals.
